2. Personal Data Collected

Offrii collects the following categories of data:

2.1 Data provided directly by the user

  • Account data: email address, display name, profile photo (optional)
  • OAuth data: when signing in via Google or Apple, we receive only your email address, name, and profile photo. We do not access any other data from your Google or Apple account.
  • Wishlists: title, description, category, priority, URL link, associated images
  • Circles: circle name, invited members
  • Community help: description of the need, category, associated images
  • Messages: content of messages exchanged within the community help feature

2.2 Data collected automatically

  • Connection logs: IP address, date and time of connection, device type and operating system
  • Session tokens: technical identifiers required to maintain your authenticated session
  • Push notification tokens: Apple APNs technical identifier for sending push notifications (if you enable them)

2.3 Data not collected

Offrii does not collect: real-time geolocation data, payment or banking information, phone contacts, browsing history outside the application, health data, or biometric data.

10. Minimum Age

Use of Offrii is restricted to individuals aged 15 years or older, in accordance with Article 45 of French Law No. 78-17 of January 6, 1978 (transposing Article 8 of the GDPR). If you are between 15 and 16 years old, you may create an account without parental consent.

If we become aware that a user is under 15 years of age, their account will be deleted and their data erased as soon as possible.

11. Data Security

Offrii implements the following technical and organizational measures to ensure the security of your personal data:

  • Encryption in transit: all communications between your device and our servers are encrypted using TLS 1.3
  • Password hashing: passwords are hashed using the Argon2 algorithm, considered the state of the art in password hashing (winner of the Password Hashing Competition)
  • Secure storage: data is stored on secure servers located within the European Union (Falkenstein, Germany)
  • Access control: data access is restricted to what is strictly necessary by the data controller
  • Security updates: regular application of security patches to the server infrastructure

12. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Offrii is a native mobile application. It does not use any tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party trackers.

The only local storage used is:

  • Authentication token: stored securely on your device (iOS Keychain) to maintain your signed-in session.
  • Web claim token: when an unauthenticated visitor claims an item via a public share page (/shared/[token]), a temporary token is stored in the browser's localStorage to allow subsequent cancellation of the claim. This token does not contain any personal data.

These storage mechanisms are strictly necessary for the operation of the service and are exempt from consent under the ePrivacy Directive (Article 5(3) of Directive 2002/58/EC).

No behavioral analysis, cross-app tracking, or targeted advertising is performed.
